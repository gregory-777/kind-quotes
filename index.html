Mission
I started kindquotes.org because I wanted to promote kindness, couragessness, and strength in the world while also not letting important quotes get lost in the fabrics of time. I've always loved seeing other people's perspective on life and I built this website as a way to see famous people's perspective on traits like courage and strength while also connecting back to the public and seeing how they perceived certain quotes. My hope is that this website inspires anyone who reads it every day and encourages them enough to share what the Quote of the Day means to them.
